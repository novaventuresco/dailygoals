Frequently Asked Questions

Is Daily Goals really free?

Yes. The app is completely free to download and use. There's an optional donation if you find it helpful, but it's not required to access any features.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your goals are stored securely in your account and are never shared, sold, or used for advertising. There's no tracking, no analytics, and no third-party access to your data.

What makes this different from other habit trackers?

Most habit trackers use streaks and gamification that make you feel guilty when you miss a day. Daily Goals resets every midnight — incomplete goals don't carry over. You focus on today's priorities without the pressure of maintaining perfect streaks.

Can I track more than 3-5 goals per day?

You can add up to 10 goals each day, but the app is designed around the idea of focusing on 3-5 top priorities each day. This helps prevent overwhelm and keeps you focused on what truly matters.

Is Daily Goals available on Android?

Not yet. Daily Goals is currently only available for iPhone. An Android version may come in the future depending on user demand.
